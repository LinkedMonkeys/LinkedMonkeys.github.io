Puzzle in Place mathcs@obu

What is this?

Puzzle in Place is a summer puzzle-solving event! For students, alumni, parents, faculty, staff, and anyone else who wants to participate, it's an opportunity to indulge in some fun and interesting puzzles (and bragging rights). For both incoming and returning MathCS students, it is also a friendly competition with a chance to earn a gift card for the OBU Bookstore!
